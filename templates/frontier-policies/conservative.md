# Frontier policy — conservative

The trust-building preset: reads are free, every local mutation asks first, compensated
external effects need expert review, and external effects without a declared compensation
simply do not run (dry-run only). Right for first sessions in a project, non-technical
owners, regulated data, or any time the cost of one bad action outweighs days of friction.
Overrides may only tighten; loosening means consciously switching to `balanced`.
